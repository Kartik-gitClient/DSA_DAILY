================================================================
KEY OBSERVATIONS:
================================================================

1. Each pass GUARANTEES largest unsorted element reaches its
   correct position at the end.

2. Each pass does ONE LESS comparison than previous pass
   because last elements are already sorted.

3. Total comparisons = 5+4+3+2+1 = 15 = n*(n-1)/2 = O(n²)

4. Why called "Bubble" Sort?
   Larger elements slowly "bubble up" to the right
   like bubbles rising to the surface of water! 🫧
